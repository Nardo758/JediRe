STR / FLIP / LAND HOLD — "NOT YET SUPPORTED" NOTICE IN SELECTOR

CONTEXT
Per STRATEGY_AND_BEFORE_AFTER_INVESTIGATION.md, these three 
strategies have zero dedicated F9 UI today:
- str_shortterm (ADR, RevPAR, seasonal occupancy not rendered)
- flip (short-hold exit timing not rendered)
- land_hold (income rows shown for a no-income strategy)

Operators who select these strategies get the multifamily-existing 
layout, which is structurally wrong for their use case. Full UI 
support is deferred. This dispatch adds an explicit "not yet 
supported" treatment so operators get an honest signal rather 
than a broken display.
