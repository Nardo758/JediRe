What's Missing: Property Identity Resolution

Right now we have:

• ✅ Municipal data (parcel, owner, year built, units, value)
• ✅ Rent data matching (Apartment Locator AI integration)
• ❌ Property identity (name, website, phone, management co, photos)

We need a Property Identity Resolver that takes a discovered property and enriches it with:

| Reviews/Rating     | Google Places                                   |
| Social Media       | Website scrape                                  |
