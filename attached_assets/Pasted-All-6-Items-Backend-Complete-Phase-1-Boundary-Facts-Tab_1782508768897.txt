🔄 How the pipeline flows now
T12 upload → data-router.ts writes extraction_t12 with months[] (Phase 0)
Seeder runs → reads actuals_through_month, acquisition_date (Phase 1), builds periodic_seed (Phase 2)
Gap detected → deriveGapForSeed applies compound trends (Phase 3)
New actuals arrive → reconcileMonth computes variance, applyRebase updates (Phase 4)
F9 requests → GET /periodic returns full series (Phase 5 backend)
Status transition → guardTransition validates, side effects fire (Phase 6)
