cells. S1-01 forbids exactly this substitution. The column may be safe ‚Äî but this log does
not prove it.

nullable. The original bug characterization (NULL-denial) was wrong; the real production

run once through the automation_level haze ‚Äî no stop, no re-review before the irreversible DDL.
