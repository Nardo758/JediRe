Two corrections to the landing page work:

1. Platform name is "JediRe" — one word, that capitalization. 
Not "JEDI RE." Update all references in the landing page, share 
resolution responses, email templates, and anywhere else the 
platform name appears.

2. "Powered by JediRe" attribution is removable for higher 
subscription tiers (paid white-label feature).

Implementation:

Subscription tier flag:
- Standard tiers (Scout, Operator): cannot remove attribution. 
  "Powered by JediRe" always renders in landing page footer 
  and any other recipient-facing surfaces.
- Premium tiers (Principal, Institutional): can remove 
  attribution. Sender chooses whether to show or hide it.

2. Identify how to add a per-tier capability: 
   can_remove_attribution boolean.
3. Identify whether the toggle should be per-share or 
   account-default. Probably account-default with optional 
   per-share override.

Sender-side UI:
- Account settings section for branding includes (for eligible 
  tiers): "JediRe Attribution" toggle. Default: visible.
- For non-eligible tiers: section shows "JediRe attribution 
  visible — upgrade to Principal or higher to customize."
- Optional per-share override in the share creation modal: 
  "Show JediRe attribution on this share" (defaults to account 
  setting).

Share resolution endpoint:
- Returns attribution_visible: boolean as part of the share 
  resolution response
- Determined by: sender's tier capability + sender's account 
  setting + per-share override (if applicable)

Landing page rendering:
- If attribution_visible is true: render "Powered by JediRe" 
  in the secondary position as currently designed
- If false: omit the attribution entirely. Header is purely 
  sender branding. Footer omits "Powered by JediRe."
- The sender's logo and company name remain regardless (those 
  aren't optional — they're the recipient's trust signal)

Default for senders on premium tiers: attribution visible. 
Removal is an active choice the sender makes, not a default. 
This preserves the JediRe brand for senders who haven't 
explicitly opted out.

- Phase B: Tier-gated attribution toggle in account settings — 
  ships for eligible tiers only
