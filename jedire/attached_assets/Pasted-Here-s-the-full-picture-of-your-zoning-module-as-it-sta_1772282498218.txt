The Agent
The ZoningAgent itself is a thin 52-line wrapper — it delegates to ZoningService which does geocoding (Google → Mapbox fallback) and PostGIS point-in-polygon queries. The real intelligence lives in the service layer.
What's Notable
The module has strong bones but some unevenness — Tab 2 (Dev Capacity) and Tab 5 (Time-to-Shovel) are fully fleshed out at ~1,200 lines each, while Tab 3 (HBU) at 249 lines looks more skeletal. The backend intelligence (recommendation orchestrator, triangulation, precedent analysis) is significantly more mature than the frontend surfaces them.
